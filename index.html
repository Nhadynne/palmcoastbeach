WHISPERING PALMS

As a well-known local travel destination in Ivisan, Palm Coast Beach Resort offers various amenities such as daytime cottages, a restaurant, coffee, and water sports activities such as kayaking and paddle boarding. As the very first accredited tourism enterprise to resume operations during the pandemic, these new attractions helped us boost our popularity all over Capiz and its nearby provinces.

GET TO KNOW MORE

"To provide an unforgettable sanctuary where guests can reconnect with nature, embrace adventure, and create lasting memories. Rooted in family values and inspired by the beauty of our coastal environment, we are committed to offering exceptional hospitality, sustainable practices, and a tranquil escape for all who visit."

"We started this resort during the challenging times of the pandemic in 2019, driven by a shared dream of creating a sanctuary where nature meets tranquility. Nestled in a picturesque coastal area, the resort was named Palm Coast because of the abundance of coconut trees that gently sway with the ocean breeze, forming a serene and refreshing canopy over the property. Each corner of the resort reflects the warmth and effort of our family, who came together to transform this lush paradise into a peaceful getaway for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure. Whether you're here to marvel at the beauty of nature, embark on exciting activities, or simply unwind under the tropical skies, Palm Coast Beach Resort offers a haven of comfort and unforgettable memories."

"To be a premier seaside resort renowned for our harmonious blend of natural beauty, outstanding service, and dedication to preserving the environment. We envision creating a global destination where guests feel rejuvenated, inspired, and connected to the wonders of the tropics."
