About Us

As a well-known local travel destination in Ivisan, Palm Coast Beach Resort offers various amenities such as daytime cottages, restaurant, coffee, and water sports activities such as kayaking and paddle boarding. As the very first accredited tourism enterprise to resume operations during pandemic, these new attractions helped us boost our popularity all over in Capiz and it's nearby provinces.
